What We Offer

Premium Rental Services

We provide everything you need for a safe, adventurous, and unforgettable road trip across Sri Lanka.

License Assistance

We help you get your Sri Lankan driving permit sorted before you arrive. Send us your international license, and we'll handle the paperwork.

Island-Wide Delivery

Start your journey anywhere. We can deliver your Tuk Tuk or Motorbike to the airport, your hotel in Colombo, or any major city in Sri Lanka.

Comprehensive Insurance

Drive with peace of mind. All our rentals come with comprehensive insurance covering you, your passengers, and third parties.

24/7 Roadside Assistance

Got a flat tire or engine trouble? Our island-wide network of mechanics will get you back on the road swiftly, anytime, anywhere.

Luggage Solutions

Our Tuk Tuks come fitted with custom luggage racks, and we offer surfboard mounts. Leave excess bags at our secure storage facility.

Driving Lesson

Never driven a Tuk Tuk? No problem! We provide a comprehensive driving lesson and safety briefing before handing over the keys.
